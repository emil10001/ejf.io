**Ingredients**

-   3 oz Scotch
-   1.5 oz Sweet Vermouth
-   Bitters
-   1 Maraschino Cherry

**Directions:**

Combine the liquid ingredients in a shaker with ice. Shake it. Pour the
results into a glass with a maraschino cherry.
